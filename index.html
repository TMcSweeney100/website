The Original Trilogy

The Curse of the Black Pearl

The adventure begins when Captain Jack Sparrow arrives in Port Royal, only to find himself entangled in a quest to reclaim his stolen ship, the Black Pearl. Meanwhile, Elizabeth Swann is taken by cursed pirates, forcing Will Turner to team up with Jack on a daring mission to save her and break the ancient Aztec curse.

Dead Man's Chest

Jack Sparrow is on the run from the legendary Davy Jones, who demands payment of a blood debt. As Will Turner and Elizabeth Swann get caught in the chaos, they all race to find the mythical Dead Man’s Chest, which holds the heart of Davy Jones—and control over the sea itself.

At World's End

With Jack trapped in Davy Jones’ Locker, Will, Elizabeth, and Barbossa must join forces to rescue him. The final battle looms as the Pirate Lords of the Brethren Court prepare for war against the East India Trading Company and its unstoppable warship, the Endeavour.

Legends of the High Seas

Set sail on a journey through the thrilling adventures of Captain Jack Sparrow and his fearless crew. From cursed treasures to legendary sea battles, the tales of the Black Pearl continue to capture the imaginations of fans worldwide.

Whether it's navigating through the Devil's Triangle or outwitting the East India Trading Company, the spirit of piracy lives on. Join us as we explore the mysteries, myths, and mischief of the Caribbean’s most notorious swashbucklers.
